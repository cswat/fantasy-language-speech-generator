Instructions

Use this tool to translate English phrases into gibberish that takes the guise of a different language, selected by you. Parameters function as follows:

  • Influences: Select one or more real languages to influence your translation. You must select at least one language.
  • More of: Enter up to 6 characters to increase their frequency in the translation.
  • Less of: Enter up to 6 characters to decrease their frequency in the translation.
  • None of: Enter up to 6 characters that will not be included in the translation. Depending on the characters and selected language(s), this may render translation impossible.
  • Enter text: Type the phrase you want translated here. If you preface a word with the "@" symbol (e.g. "@Douglas"), the word will not be translated.

The designer's intended application of this tool is for use in generating fantasy languages. An example is listed below.

Dwarvish:

  • Influences: Danish, German, Swedish
  • More of: B, R
  • Less of: Z

"I am a Dwarf and I'm digging a hole." becomes "Ola ka li Ben nu Ard'en arla in ile."
